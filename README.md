## What is Kubera?

**Kubera** is named after **Kubera** — the Hindu god of wealth, treasury, and riches. He is the divine banker of the gods, the guardian of treasures, and the lord of all riches. Just like the mythological Kubera watched over the wealth of the heavens, **Kubera the app watches over every rupee of yours** — tracking, organizing, and helping you understand where your money goes.

Kubera is a **personal finance tracker** built for real people with real money problems. It's not another boring spreadsheet or a complicated banking app. It's **your personal money companion** that helps you answer the one question we all ask:

> *"Where did all my money go?"*

## Why is Kubera Unique?

### It Speaks Your Language

No corporate jargon. No "Income" and "Expense." Kubera uses words you actually say in real life:

- **"Got"** — When money comes in
- **"Spent"** — When money goes out

Because that's how you think about money. Not in accounting terms. In *your* terms.

### Built for India, Made for Everyone

- **Indian Number System** — ₹1,23,456 not $123,456
- **UPI First** — UPI is the default payment method because that's how India pays
- **Multi-Currency** — Supports ₹, $, €, £ if you need it
- **Indian Payment Methods** — UPI, Cash, Card, Net Banking — all recognized

### Premium Design, Zero Clutter

- **Glassmorphism UI** — Beautiful frosted-glass cards on a warm cream-to-peach gradient
- **Mobile-First** — Designed for your phone, not your desktop
- **Smooth Animations** — Count-up numbers, stagger transitions, ripple effects, swipe gestures
- **Dark & Warm Color Palette** — Burnt orange, forest green, soft cream — easy on the eyes

### Lightweight & Fast

No bloat. No slowdowns. Kubera is built to be **lightweight and snappy** — opens instantly, loads data fast, and never gets in your way.

## Features That Actually Matter

### Dashboard

Your financial command center at a glance:

- **Net Balance** with animated count-up display
- **Quick-Add** — Tap "Got" or "Spent" to log in 3 seconds
- **Recent Transactions** — Your last 5 moves, always visible

### Transactions (Full Control)

- **Create, Edit, Delete** — Full control over every entry
- **Search** — Find any transaction by purpose, person, or note
- **Filter Everything** — By type, payment method, time period, or custom date range
- **Autocomplete** — Kubera remembers your past entries and suggests them as you type
- **Swipe to Edit** — Swipe left on any transaction to reveal edit/delete buttons
- **Pagination** — 20 transactions per page with "Load More"

### Insights (Know Your Money)

Four powerful charts that reveal your financial patterns:

1. **Income vs Spending** — Grouped bar chart (daily, weekly, monthly views)
2. **Where Does Your Money Go?** — Donut chart showing your top spending categories
3. **How Do You Pay?** — Donut chart showing UPI vs Cash vs Card usage
4. **Balance Over Time** — Line chart tracking your net worth trend
5. **Stat Cards** — Biggest expense, biggest income, most frequent purpose, most used method

### Reports (Weekly, Monthly, Yearly)

Professional-grade financial reports that would make your CA proud:

- **Weekly** — Day-by-day breakdown, top spending day, previous week comparison
- **Monthly** — Week-by-week view, best saving week, top 5 categories
- **Yearly** — Quarter comparison, month-by-month summary, best & worst months
- **Comparison** — See how this week/month/year compares to the last one
- **Transaction Table** — Full list with filterable type cards (All/Got/Spent)

### Data Export (Take Your Data Anywhere)

- **PDF Export** — Professional A4 reports with cover page, branded headers/footers, colored summary cards
- **Excel Export** — Multi-sheet workbook with 4 tabs: Summary, Transactions, By Purpose, By Payment Method
- **CSV Export** — Standard format for spreadsheets and data analysis
- **Export Any Period** — Week, month, year, or all transactions

### Built-in Calculator

- **Quick Math** — Calculate tips, splits, or totals without leaving the app
- **Money Formatting** — Results shown in Indian number system with currency symbol
- **History** — Last 50 calculations saved automatically

### Settings (Make It Yours)

- **Currency Selection** — ₹, $, €, £
- **Default Transaction Type** — Pre-select "Got" or "Spent"
- **Default Payment Method** — Pre-select your most-used method
- **Profile Management** — Edit your display name
- **Danger Zone** — Delete all data with type-to-confirm safeguard

## How Kubera Helps You

### Problem: "I Don't Know Where My Money Goes"

**Kubera's Solution:** Log every transaction in seconds. The Insights dashboard shows you exactly where your money goes with interactive charts. See your top 5 spending categories. See which payment method you use most. See your balance trend over time.

### Problem: "I Forget What I Spent On"

**Kubera's Solution:** Search any transaction by purpose, person, or note. Autocomplete remembers your past entries so you don't have to type everything again. Filter by date, type, or payment method to find anything instantly.

### Problem: "I Need Reports for Tax/CA/Budgeting"

**Kubera's Solution:** Generate professional weekly, monthly, or yearly reports. Export them as PDF, Excel, or CSV. The PDFs look like they came from a financial firm — with cover pages, branded headers, and colored summary cards.

### Problem: "Existing Apps Are Too Complicated"

**Kubera's Solution:** Kubera uses plain language ("Got" and "Spent"), has a clean glassmorphism UI, and works perfectly on your phone. No learning curve. Open it, tap "Got" or "Spent," enter the amount, done.

### Problem: "I Need Quick Calculations"

**Kubera's Solution:** Built-in calculator with money formatting and history. Calculate tips, split bills, or do quick math without switching apps.
